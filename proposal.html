What I'll Do For You

The following sections outline the scope of work for this project.

Phase 1: Discovery and Wireframes

I'll do a series of questionnaires and/or phone interviews to gather your requirements and goals for your web platform. You'll provide your content (text, photographs, logos, etc.) to help me determine pages your site will include. Once this is decided, I'll complete rough, black-and-white wireframes for each page to determine the overall layout of your site's content. Once you approve the wireframes, we'll move on to Phase 2.

Phase 2: Design

First, we'll decide together on a color template for your site. Once the color template is decided, I'll mock-up your site's homepage in a static design including content, colors, typography and layout based on the wireframes from Phase 1. Once you approve of the final static design, we'll move on to Phase 3.

Phase 3: Development

In this phase, I'll implement the rest of the pages on the site by converting the static designs into a functional website. Also, I'll set up your included e-commerce (http://www.woothemes.com/woocommerce/) and support chat (http://wp-livechat.com/) plugins.

In Summary

My primary goal is to help you have a high-quality, functional website that helps you make a good first impression on your visitors. My secondary goal is to do this on-time and on-budget. I understand how important it is for you to make a return on your investment.
